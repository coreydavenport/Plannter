What is Plannter?

Developed for

Trellis Root Farm & Apiary

Plannter is a scheduling and data visualization application, uniquely designed for farmers and gardeners of all skill levels, to assist with remembering when to plant and transplant seeds.

Set your number of seed generations, custom plant period, and transplant period, and Plannter will autopopulate the calendar with easy to understand events that take all the work out of scheduling, which gives you more time to do what you love. Grow.
